🧠 Core Visual Summaries

1. Concept Map: Basic Cell Structure and Function

Cell Membrane

Controls entry and exit of substances. Think of it as the cell's security guard, deciding what gets in and what stays out.

Nucleus

Contains genetic information (DNA). The cell's control center, like a computer's CPU directing all activities.

Mitochondria

Energy (ATP) production. The cell's power plant, converting nutrients into usable energy.

Metabolism

Chemical processes for energy, growth, and waste elimination. The cell's factory operations.

3. Key Muscles and Bones for Estheticians

Frontalis Muscle

Raises eyebrows and creates forehead wrinkles. Important for facial expressions and anti-aging treatments.

Orbicularis Oculi

Closes eyes and creates crow's feet. Key area for eye treatments and wrinkle prevention.

Platysma

Lowers jaw and affects neck appearance. Important for neck and jawline treatments.

Cranium & Facial Bones

Provide structure for facial features. Understanding bone structure helps in contouring and massage techniques.
